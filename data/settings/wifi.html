Change WiFi

You have two ways to change the WiFi connection:

  1. Enter the new WiFi details manually
    You can type in the name (SSID) and password of the new WiFi. But keep in mind: the device will restart and try to connect to it. If the connection fails, it will go back to the current WiFi.
  2. Use the setup hotspot method
    PandaGarage can restart and create its own temporary WiFi hotspot (like it did during the first setup). You'll need to be near the device and connect to this hotspot with your phone or computer to choose the new WiFi network.
